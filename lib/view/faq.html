How does Noirdoor work?


Artists:

• You will earn $7.00USD/Month per supporting fan. (100 Fans supporting you on Noirdoor = $700.00USD/Month for you) • Each time a fan decides to support you, the duration of his/her support will last for 1 month and will automatically renew each month. • If the fan decides to support a different artist , the $7.00USD/Month will switch to the new supported artist. • If a new fan discovers you through a Noirdoor Fan Page and decides to support you, you will earn $6.00USD/Month and $1.00USD will go to the Noirdoor Fan through which you were discovered. • It is your responsability to maintain a real relationship with your fans by creating the best music and reward them in the most original way. This will attract new fans and maintain your current Noirdoor fan-base. Fans: • Each time an artist gets a new supporting fan through your Noirdoor fan page, you will earn $1.00USD/Month. You only need 10 new supporting fan through your Noirdoor fan page to zero out your membership! • The duration of this earning will last as long as the new supporter is supporting the specific artist he/she discovered through your Noirdoor fan page. • If the new supporter decides to support a different artist through someone else's Noirdoor fan page, the $1.00USD/Month will switch to the new recommender.

Fans:

• Each time an artist gets a new supporting fan through your Noirdoor fan page, you will earn $1.00USD/Month. You only need 10 new supporting fan through your Noirdoor fan page to zero out your membership! • The duration of this earning will last as long as the new supporter is supporting the specific artist he/she discovered through your Noirdoor fan page. • If the new supporter decides to support a different artist through someone else's Noirdoor fan page, the $1.00USD/Month will switch to the new recommender.

COPYRIGHTS & CONTENT OWNERSHIP


Do I own my content on Noirdoor?

Except granting a license to us to perform and promote Noirdoor's Service, you own your own content. We claim no intellectual property rights over the material you provide to the Service. Your profile and materials uploaded remain yours. However, by publishing your content on Noirdoor, you agree to allow others to view your content. In any case, we will not enable 3rd party advertisement on your content.

Is my content protected?

Noirdoor works closely with ASCAP & BMI to make sure your content is copyrighted.

My content is not protected, what should I do?

We highly recomend that you create an ASCAP or BMI profile and get an ASCAP or BMI id# in order to make sure that your content is copyrighted. Note sure how to do it? we are here to help!

I am signed on a Label, can I still use Noirdoor?

Unsigned/independent Artists are more than welcome to use Noirdoor. It is for you that Noirdoor was created. If you are signed on a label, you need to make sure that your contract is non-exclusive and that you own your copyrights in order to avoid any copyright infringement.

How is my music used by Noirdoor?

Once you create your Noirdoor Artist Profile and upload your music, your music will be streamable by the Noirdoor Community. Meanwhile, you do have the option to make your music available only to your Noirdoor Supporting Fans.

Will I earn royalties if my music is played on Noirdoor?

Noirdoor is ASCAP and BMI Licensed. This mean that we do pay royalties to the ASCAP and BMI organizations for the difusion of music on the Noirdoor website. It is your responsability to make sure that your music is protected through The ASCAP or The BMI organization in order to receive your cut.

Can I upload any song on Noirdoor?

No. Noirdoor was created to help the preservation of the cultural diversity brought by independent creators. Our goal is to make Noirdoor "The Place to Go" to discover the very best new original music by independent artists. ​ For the time being we only accept ORIGINAL MUSIC and DO NOT ACCEPT COVER SONGS.

What is the difference between "Public Album" and "Private Album"?

"Public Album" means that your album will be available to the noirdoor community as soon as it is done uploading. "Private Album" allow you to keep your album private and set a release date for it. The album will be available to the noirdoor community on the chosen date.

What does "Make my music discoverable" means?

By clicking "Make my music discoverable" your music will automatically be added to the Noirdoor "DISCOVER" feature. This means that your music will be discoverable by anyone using the "DISCOVER' feature, Noirdoor member or not.

What does "Exclusive" means?

"Exclusive" means that your content will be available exclusively to your Noirdoor Supporting Fans on Noirdoor.

What does "New" means?

"New" stands for new content created.

REWARDS


What are rewards?

Rewards are for your Noirdoor Supporting Fans! Think of this as a special treat for your fans that decide to support you. A lot of Noirdoor Artists release bonus content early, host google hangouts, or ask their Noirdoor Supporting Fans for ideas for their next creation. It's really up to you! We encourage Noirdoor Artists to search around and see what other Noirdoor Artists are offering their Noirdoor Supporting Fans. You can use the same rewards, but with your own touch or ask your fans what they would like! Rewards aren't required but are a great way to maintain your current supporting fan base, keep them happy and get new ones.

Can I edit my rewards?

Of course you can! You can edit existing rewards, delete reward or create additional rewards at any time.

How do I distribute my rewards?

There are a couple ways to distribute rewards on Noirdoor. You can either post them to your Noirdoor Artist page or send messages via your "Notifications" Tab. We recommend posting rewards using your Noirdoor Artist page, as it's an easy way to upload/attach media and share it with your Noirdoor Fans. You can share URLs, images, attachments and then select the privacy by choosing which Noirdoor Fams will have access to your new posts.

PAYMENTS


When do I get charged?

For monthly payment plans, service is billed in advance on a monthly basis. All credit card payments are secured and verified using Stripe. We don’t accept cash, checks, or PayPal for payment. Also, please note that we bill in USD and international payments may be subject to conversion fees.

When do I get paid?

Monthly.

What are my options to receive payment?

You have the option to receive payment via Stripe direct deposit to your checking account. Transfer fees: Receiving payment via Stripe - $0.25 fee for every deposit.

How do you calculate fees?

Our mission at Noirdoor is to send as much money to you as possible, so we do everything we can to minimize fees. We hope this article sheds some light on the three main types. Payment processing fees represent the amount that our payment processors (e.g., Stripe) charge each time they process a payment. Payment processors typically charge a percentage of each support plus a fixed number of cents.Noirdoor reduces processing fees by aggregating charges at the beginning of each month. International transfer feesInternational transfer fees apply to payments that pass between two different countries. Receiving payment via Stripe - $0.25 fee for every deposit Note: This does not include any fees associated with converting USD to your native currency.
